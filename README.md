## 🔭 Projects

### [Credit Risk & Stress Testing Model](https://github.com/kabirguglani/credit-risk-model)
Full credit risk pipeline on 1,000 consumer loans — PD scoring, Expected Loss (PD × LGD × EAD), and macro stress testing across 5 scenarios (GFC, COVID, rising rates). Built to Basel II IRB framework.
- **AUC-ROC: 0.833** · Gini: 0.666 · 5-fold CV AUC: 0.806 ± 0.019
- GFC scenario increases Expected Loss by **+109%** · CCC-rated loans +71%
- WoE/IV feature engineering · Logistic regression scorecard · Python + Excel report

### [Multi-Factor Smart Beta Portfolio](https://github.com/kabirguglani/factor-portfolio)
Rules-based equity strategy selecting the top 30 S&P 500 stocks monthly using value, momentum, and quality factor scores. 2018–2024 backtest across 501 securities.
- **47% reduction in max drawdown** vs S&P 500 equal-weight benchmark (-12.6% vs -23.9%)
- Annualised volatility 15.3% vs 17.3% · Beta 0.75 · Sortino ratio 1.02
- Built in Python: yfinance · pandas · scipy · matplotlib

### [AI Tone Bias in Earnings Call Summaries](https://github.com/kabirguglani/ai-tone-bias)
Compared GPT-4 vs Claude Sonnet earnings call summaries against professional analyst reports (NVIDIA, P&G). Identified systematic tone divergence producing conflicting investment recommendations from identical transcripts.
- Claude rated P&G **4x more negatively** than GPT-4 (tone score: −11 vs −3)
- Same transcript, different AI → **HOLD vs REDUCE** recommendation
- Classified 5 semantic failure modes beyond hallucination

### [Equity Valuation — Applied Materials (AMAT)](https://github.com/kabirguglani/amat-valuation)
Full equity valuation of AMAT using DCF, Comparable Companies, and Precedent Transactions. CFA Research Challenge, Temple University.
- Target price: **$250.00** · Upside: **27.55%** vs market price of $197.20
- WACC: 10.09% · Perpetual growth: 4.5% · 3-scenario analysis (Bull/Base/Bear)
- Tools: Excel · FactSet · Capital IQ
